# Ireland University Logos - Download Guide

## Instructions
Download each logo and save it to: `public/universities/` with the exact filename specified.

## 1. Dublin Business School (DBS)
**Filename:** `dublin-business-school.png`

**Option 1 - Official Website:**
1. Visit: https://www.dbs.ie/
2. Right-click on the DBS logo at the top
3. Save image as `dublin-business-school.png`

**Option 2 - Logo Database:**
1. Visit: https://brandfetch.com/dbs.ie
2. Click on the logo
3. Download PNG version
4. Save as `dublin-business-school.png`

**Option 3 - Direct Search:**
- Google Image Search: "Dublin Business School logo PNG transparent"
- Look for 1200x780 or higher resolution
- Save as `dublin-business-school.png`

   - Download logo in PNG format

   - Search for university name + "logo"
   - Download PNG preview of SVG files
